About Me

Full-Stack Developer

I am a full-stack software developer trained to finesse at microverse.org and specialize in building software solutions for small businesses. I love coding specifically because I love the puzzle-solving aspect of it. Although I have predominantly been working with JavaScript and Ruby, I have a fair bit of experience with C, C++, Python, and Java and have had the opportunity to work with Matlab and Arduino.
I have built over 40 different projects with different technologies and stacks. These include a full-stack Ruby on Rails budget web app: Spend Right, which enables users to manage their finances by keeping all their transactions in categories and tracking how much they spend in each category and on each expense.
I have also had the honor to work as a code reviewer at Microverse, where I have reviewed more than 50 different codebases, enforcing DRY and clean code and helping students resolve bugs and semantic errors, drastically improving the efficiency and performance of their codebases and getting them unblocked on their projects.
